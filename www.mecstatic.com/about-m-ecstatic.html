About//???//Requests



M!ecstatic is a handmade collection by Michelle Sanchez, based out of Houston, Texas.

I have always been in love with microscopic details.

My endeavors in jewelry began by accident in 2011 after pulling an all-nighter crafting during Thanksgiving break while no one else was home. My first jewelry designs were full of intricate beading and left over scraps of metal. After receiving positive feedback for those designs, I decided to keep it going. Anything I could twist and bend became part of my work. Once I learned to manipulate metal with fire, I lept into another world, and I am still learning as I go!

My goal for M!ecstatic is to create unique jewelry for all kinds of people. Although most pieces can be replicated, I tend to steer away from mass production because I want to share as many designs as my hands and mind can conjure.


Please email me for questions or requests.

If you have a design in mind and need help executing your dream, I am here to help!

I also offer soldering and repair services for silver and brass.
